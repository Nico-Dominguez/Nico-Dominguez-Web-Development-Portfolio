A little bit about me!

I've always been undecided of what I want to do in life. This indecision has plagued me even through college. I was always a go with the flow type of guy. When I got my first job I was excited! Why? because I finally got a job doing what I learned at college? Maybe... Because I had a stable income? Most Likely.

By year three, I was yearning for something new. I had come to a conclusion, I did not like what I was doing... I have always had a creative mind but my job was limiting that. This is when I discovered computer programming. This new realm, albeit a bit more difficult intrigued me and I wanted to learn more. I took a simple html, css course and I fell in love.

I am now a self-taught frontend developer with an itch to learn more. I'm currently looking for frontend devloper roles and also freelance. Hopefully we can connect!
